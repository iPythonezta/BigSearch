From Silicon to Startups: A Conversation with Pat Gelsinger

Pat Gelsinger

General Partner Playground Global and former CEO, Intel

Intel legend and newly minted investor Pat Gelsinger joins StrictlyVC for a wide-ranging conversation about leadership, legacy, and the future of deep tech. From steering a Fortune 500 giant to mentoring founders at Playground Global, Gelsinger shares hard-won lessons on building enduring technology, why deep tech doesn’t fit the traditional VC mold, and how the U.S. can stay competitive in a world where chips have become the new oil.

6:30PM – 7:00PM

Beyond the Interface: Building Technology That Extends Human Thought

Mina Fahmi

Co-founder and CEO Sandbar

Toni Schneider

Partner True Ventures

True Ventures partner Toni Schneider joins Sandbar co-founder and CEO Mina Fahmi to discuss the next evolution of human–computer interaction. Fahmi’s company is behind Stream, a conversational interface that acts as an extension of your thinking, and Stream Ring, a “mouse for voice.” Together they’ll explore how founders can design technology that enhances focus, creativity, and cognition — not just productivity.
